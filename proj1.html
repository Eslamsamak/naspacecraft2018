Launch Vehicle

What is a Launch Vehicle? A launch vehicle is what most of us think of as a "rocket." It provides the velocity needed by a spacecraft to escape Earth's gravity and set it on its course for Mars.

The InSight mission was designed to launch at a time when Earth and Mars are in positions in their orbits that are advantageous for spacecraft traveling to and landing on Mars. This also allows the spacecraft to get to Mars faster than would be possible otherwise. This takes about six months for InSight.
