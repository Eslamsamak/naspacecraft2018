We Have Mission in Nasa Space Apps 2018

Mission to the Moon.. The aim of the project is to launch a rover to the moon in an area in which we can study so much. This can be done by the following scenario: The landing site is determined through various concerns like the ability of the rover to land on a smooth surface without crashing, the ability for the rover to travel safely on the moon and the complexity of the geology in the area to be studied. The rocket’s trajectory is determined by celestial mechanics and rocket’s mechanics. The rocket is launched from Earth, putting in concern the launching site’s consequences like the explosion, the payload.. etc.
